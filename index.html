OUR PAST PROJECTS

Kritika International Trade is a contractor company having a history of catering to the unique requirements of the army and supplying and serving the men in uniform with high quality and critical top-notch medical equipment over the years. The company feels honored to have played an important role to support the armed forces with the good quality equipment. The company’s collaborations with the armed forced underscores the dedication for providing top quality and reliable materials and equipment. The company has provided the army with top notch medical equipment like field medical packs, diagnostic equipment such as portable X-ray machines and ultrasound machines and also personal protective equipment like masks, gloves and other protective gear. These are a few among the many equipment supplied by the company to the army for their welfare. Kritika International Trade also provides top-class tactical equipment to meet the stringent requirements of the army. The company understands the critical nature of the army’s operations, and hence is dedicated to provide the best quality gear and services to the men in uniform ensuring their safety and effectiveness. The company leverages its extensive network of trusted suppliers and manufacturers and sources the latest and the most advanced tactical gear. The company is providing the army with state-of-the-art equipment tactical and protective gear like helmets, gloves, boots, masks and ballistic vests and also backpacks along with various other military equipment. Having been founded with a commitment to excellence, the company has truly been a cornerstone in the industry.
