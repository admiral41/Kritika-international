OUR PAST PROJECTS

Kritika International Trade, a distinguished contractor company, has a rich history of meeting the distinctive needs of the military. Specializing in supplying high-quality medical equipment, the company takes pride in its longstanding commitment to serving the men and women in uniform. Collaborating closely with the armed forces, Kritika International Trade exemplifies dedication to delivering reliable materials. The company's contributions include field medical packs, diagnostic tools such as portable X-ray and ultrasound machines, as well as personal protective equipment like masks and gloves. Additionally, Kritika International Trade excels in providing top-tier tactical gear to meet the rigorous demands of the military. Recognizing the critical nature of military operations, the company ensures the safety and effectiveness of the armed forces by sourcing state-of-the-art equipment, including helmets, gloves, boots, masks, ballistic vests, backpacks, and various other military essentials. Founded on a commitment to excellence, Kritika International Trade stands as a cornerstone in the industry.
